class KHLASingleBoloSubRecord: public KSingleBoloSubRecord


 KHLASingleBoloSubRecord.cxx
 KDataStructure

 Author: Adam Cox <mailto:adam.cox@ik.fzk.de> on 3/25/10.

 * Copyright 2010 Karlsruhe Institute of Technology. All rights reserved.

 The HLA class for Single Bolo Sub Records. It stores a significant amount
 of information for each recorded bolometer event. It contains all of the
 calculated values from the ERA processing. See the descriptions below
 of each of the parameters.

private:
Double32_tfBaselineIonFiducialbaseline noise amplitude of the fiducial signal (the weighted average of the fEnergyCollectrode)
Double32_tfBaselineNoiseCollectrode[2]baseline noise amplitude on the collectrodes (in what units...)
Double32_tfBaselineNoiseGuard[2]baseline noise amplitude on the guard channels
Double32_tfBaselineNoiseHeatbaseline noise amplitude of the heat signal. Call GetBaselineNoiseHeat() to get this value.
Double32_tfBaselineNoiseVeto[2]baseline noise amplitude on the veto channels
Int_tfChi2Flagset to 1 if the chi2 of all relevant channesl is okay (different from cuts.CutChi2)
TBitsfCutsset the TestCutsBitNumber method for a list of which bit corresponds to which cut.
Double32_tfEnergyBaselineCollectrode[2]fitted value of the energy along the baseline (at "zero time") on each collectrode
Double32_tfEnergyBaselineGuard[2]fitted value of the energy along the baseline at zero time on each guard channel
Double32_tfEnergyBaselineHeatfitted value of the energy along the baseline (at "zero time") on the heat channel
Double32_tfEnergyBaselineVeto[2]fitted value of the energy along the baseline at zero time on each veto electrode
Double32_tfEnergyCollectrode[2]energy of pulse on the two collectrodes (or fiducial electrodes)
Double32_tfEnergyGuard[2]energy of pulse on the two guard channels.
Double32_tfEnergyHeatindependent of event category. based upon the heat pulse height.
Double32_tfEnergyIoncalculation based upon event category.
Double32_tfEnergyIonFiducialenergy of the fiducial signal (the weighted average of the fEnergyCollectrode)
Double32_tfEnergyIonSumthe sum of energy on all ionization channels that have a signal (calucaled by ERA)
Double32_tfEnergyRecoilcalculation based upon event category
Double32_tfEnergyVeto[2]energy of pulse on the two veto electrodes
Int_tfEventFlag0 = noise, 1 = heat only, 2 = fiducial, 3 = surface, 4 = pure guard, 5 = other.. calculated by ERA
Double_tfFiducialVolumethe value of the fiducial volume, in grams, for this detector.
Int_tfIonFlags[6]each integer in this array corresponds to a particular ion channel (collectrode 1, 2, veto 1, 2, and guard 1, 2). When the value is set to one, there was a pulse seen on that channel by ERA.
Int_tfIonPulseTimeOffsetthe time offset of the peak of the ion pulse relative to the peak of the heat pulse, in units of stamp (10 microseonds)
Double32_tfQvaluecalculation based upon event category (heat, fiducial, surface, etc...)
TReffSambaRecordpoints to the samba record associated with this event.
Int_tfTriggerBit1Samba's Liste1 event trigger bit map
Int_tfTriggerBit2Samba's Liste2 event trigger bit map
Int_tfVoltageFlagSet to one if the polarity on this bolometer is reveresed.
